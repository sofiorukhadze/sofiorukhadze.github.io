Paris... the city of love, the city of culture, the city for everyone who is trying to find a beauty in this life.And during autumn Paris is also city of rain, city of mud and city of cold weather.
Even though it is pretty cold during November Paris is still as beautiful as it gets.
Starting from beautiful, bewitching sunsets ending with afternoon walks with hot coffee and croissant, this huge city can offer some of the best views.
The center is always full of tourists and street musician who give the city its main score, folowing you everywhere after hearing it.
The museums are full of tourists, but you can still enjoy the view of your favorite paintings and what is the most important, you can find a museum in every corner of the city
First thing that you should definitely try in Paris is their amazing food and of course wine. The Mussels are pretty popular dining option in the city and you can find them practically everywhere.
Go on the top of Arc de Triomphe to see the best view of the city, take a walk in Tuileries garden and feed the ducks. Do not forget to go to Montmarte which was the home for the best painters.
Enjoy paris and come back home full of impressions and love.
