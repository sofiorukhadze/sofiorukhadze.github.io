SinceI have heard stories about its beauty, its old bridges and cathedrals have been on my mind, I have always had one thought: Prague was the most beautiful town in Europe.
So on my first year of studying in Europe I decided to spend my spring break in this magical place and finally make my big dream come true.
Prague is the best place for art, music and literature lovers. The beautiful colors of its sall old town remind you of the paintings by Van Gogh.
Prague smells like beer, oil and Trdelnik (spit cake) and there is music, music everywhere. Every corner is full of street musicians who try to play their best songs.
Charles bridge, Kafka museum, astronomical clock and many old cathedrals are the main attractions you should defifinitely see during your stay.
If you want to see the real beauty of Charles bridge, do not hesitate to wake up a bit earlier and take a walk through empty bridge. Prague will bring music and culture to your soul.
It will fill you with eternal happiness which will follow you everywhere as you will remember that you spent several days in Prague.
