Summer in New-York

New York, the city whichnever sleeps, city which is full of life, lights,
laughter and busy people.Where you can see places which you read about or saw on a big screens, where nobody cares who you are,
what you wear or whereare you going. Summer in New-York city can be pretty hot, but at the same time the beauty and rhythm of the city
makes you forget how hot it actually is.If you want to explore big city, see multiple museums with the greatest masterpieces
and want to see hundreds of skyscrapers reaching for the clouds,this is city for you.Take a walk on a times square and see the charisma of it with your own eyes, grab some coffee and food,
take a walk in a central park.Relax, feed squarells and read your favorite book.Go to MOMA and see starry night and on your way back go to MET and get lost in hundreds of rooms full of art.
Do not forget to go up on empire state building to see noisy New-York from the clouds and feel a little peace.
Go to the battery park and take a ferry to liberty islands to see one and only Lady Liberty.
And of course cross the brooklyn bridge to see bewitching view.
Travel to New York, because it will surprise you in many ways and become a part of you forever.
